Collect the phones

  • High Concept: Collect 10 phones without getting caught
  • Player Experience Goals: control a character and collect phones by avoiding two enemies who try to catch you
  • Genre: chasing game
  • Game play: The player can move right, left and jump and he can collect phones. There is a score on the top , and only one phone on the map at any time. Once a phone is collected another one will appear.Two enemies will try to catch you wile you collect the phones. Dont get caught!
  • Prototype goal: Basic controls and strategy to run away
  • Features: fun and simple
  • Setting/Premise: A basic enviromnent with some platforms
  • Story: Once 10 phones are collected the character can make the call and he wins
  • Target Audience: Everyone
  • Play time: about 3 minute
  • Strategies: avoid getting caught. The two enemies will move depending on how you move so try to make them go away from the phone before you approach it
